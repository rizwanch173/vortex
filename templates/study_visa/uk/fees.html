Understanding the Costs

When budgeting for your UK Student Visa application, it is important to understand that there are two separate categories of cost: the official government fees set by UKVI, and the service fee charged by Vortex Ease for our guidance and support. These are entirely separate - Vortex Ease does not collect any UKVI fees on your behalf, and our service fee only covers the support we provide. Below is a transparent breakdown of both.

Government Fees (UKVI)

Note: All government fees are set by UKVI and are subject to change. Please refer to the official UKVI website for current figures. Vortex Ease does not collect these fees.

  • Student Visa Application Fee: The UKVI Student Visa application fee is set by the UK government. As of the current policy, international students applying from outside the UK pay a specified government application fee. This fee is payable directly to UKVI when completing your online application form. The exact current fee is published on the official gov.uk website - please verify the latest figure before applying, as fees are subject to change.
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS): The Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) is a mandatory charge paid alongside your visa application. It grants you access to NHS services during your time in the UK. The IHS is calculated based on the total length of your visa - for students, it covers the full course duration plus any additional visa period granted. The current IHS rate for students per year of the visa is published on the gov.uk IHS calculator. These rates are subject to change and you should verify the current figure at the time of your application.
  • Biometric Appointment (UKVCAS): After submitting your application online, you will need to attend a biometric appointment at a UKVCAS centre. Standard appointments are typically available at no additional charge. Enhanced (premium) appointment slots - which allow greater flexibility in location and timing - carry an additional fee set by UKVCAS. Vortex Ease will advise you on which appointment type is most appropriate for your timeline.

Other Costs to Budget For

Beyond the UKVI fees and the Vortex Ease service fee, there are several other costs you may need to budget for. None of these involve Vortex Ease - they are third-party costs you manage directly.

  • English language test fees - IELTS Academic, TOEFL iBT, PTE Academic, or equivalent (fees vary by provider and location)
  • Document translation and certified translation costs if any of your documents are not in English
  • Travel costs to your nearest UKVCAS biometric appointment centre
